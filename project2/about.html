Our Story

Escapade was founded in June, 2020 in Nigeria, Escapade is a small Nigerian start-up that is going to be one of the world’s leading digital travel companies. Escapade mission is to make it easier for everyone to experience the world.

By giving all our best information about fun and beautiul places in NigeriaEscapade is going to a travel company that connects millions of travelers to memorable experiences, a variety of transportation options, and incredible places to stay – from homes to hotels, and much more.

Escapade also offers travel Guide services, by creating jobs for youths in our country. More than 50,000 accommodation listings, including over 600 guest house and hostels, apartments, and other unique places to stay. Wherever you want to go and whatever you want to do, Escapede makes it easy and supports you with 24/7 customer support..
