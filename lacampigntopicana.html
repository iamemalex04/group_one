GATE FEE


The entrance fee at La Campagne Tropicana for Adults is 5,000 NGN without corkage and 6,000 NGN with corkage.

La Campagne Tropicana Gate Fee for children is 1,500 NGN with corkage and without corkage.

The gate fee for Teenagers is 2,500 NGN without corkage and 3,000 NGN with corkage
